About Us

In 1959, two young entreprenuers moved to the United States from the Netherlands in search of living the american dream. After working for multiple companies, they decided they wanted to become their own business owners doing the thing they were pationate about, Cycling. In 1973, Gus and Chris Baanders opened up their first bicycle shop turning into a successful business devoted to professionalism and expertise. After changing ownership and 18 years later, Midwest Cyclery is back in the family. May 2015, Bob and Christina Baanders-Decker continue the same values as Gus and Chris did back in the beginning.
